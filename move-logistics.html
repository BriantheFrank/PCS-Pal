How to Use Move Logistics

Use this page as the working timeline for your move. Start with the family itinerary, then fill in packers dates and any custom events.

Family Itinerary & Calendar

  1. Enter your departure location and date.
  2. Add each overnight stop in order, with lodging details if available.
  3. Set your final destination and arrival date.
  4. Review the calendar and treat it as the authoritative timeline.
  5. Update itinerary entries so the calendar stays accurate.

Packers & Move Logistics

  1. Add the day or date range the packers will be on site.
  2. Confirm packer dates do not overlap with travel days.
  3. Use the calendar during coordination calls to avoid conflicts.

Custom Events & Notes

  1. Add appointments, vehicle drop-off, lease termination, or school enrollment.
  2. Store key contacts and reminders next to each event.
  3. Confirm custom events appear on the master calendar.

Tip: Keep this page open during calls so everyone works from the same dates.
