Army Duty Station

Fort Campbell, Kentucky/Tennessee

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Campbell is a bi-state Army installation on the Kentucky-Tennessee border that supports air assault, special operations aviation, and regional sustainment missions.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Welcome Center or Soldier Support Center for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

The Fort Campbell commissary provides DeCA grocery services for eligible patrons.

PX / Exchange

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • 101st Airborne Division (Air Assault)
  • 5th Special Forces Group (Airborne)
  • 160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment (Airborne)
  • Blanchfield Army Community Hospital
