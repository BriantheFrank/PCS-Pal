Army Duty Station

Fort Huachuca, Arizona

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Huachuca is an Army installation in southeastern Arizona that supports intelligence, network, and training missions for the Army and joint force.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • U.S. Army Intelligence Center of Excellence

  • 111th Military Intelligence Brigade
