Army Duty Station

Fort Leonard Wood, Missouri

U.S. Army Installation

Fort Leonard Wood is a central Missouri installation that supports engineer, chemical, and military police training as part of the Maneuver Support Center of Excellence.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • Maneuver Support Center of Excellence
  • U.S. Army Engineer School
  • U.S. Army Chemical, Biological, Radiological, and Nuclear School
  • U.S. Army Military Police School
