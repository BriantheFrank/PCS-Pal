Army Duty Station

Joint Base Lewis-McChord, Washington

Joint Base Installation

Joint Base Lewis-McChord (JBLM) is a joint Army-Air Force installation in western Washington that supports corps-level operations, airlift, and joint readiness missions.

Post / Installation Information

In-Processing / Reception

Arriving Soldiers report to the Welcome Center or Soldier Support Center for in-processing and administrative intake.

Commissary

JBLM commissaries on Lewis Main and McChord provide DeCA grocery services for eligible patrons.

JBLM Commissary

PX / Exchange

AAFES exchange facilities on Lewis Main and McChord Field provide retail and service options.

Major Unit Areas

Major unit areas include:

  • I Corps
  • 7th Infantry Division
  • 1st Special Forces Group (Airborne)
  • 62nd Airlift Wing (USAF)
