How to Use Move Inventory

Keep the packing guidance nearby, but collapse it when you just need to add rooms or items.

Build a complete household inventory that matches how movers pack and how claims are processed.

  1. Create one entry for every room, including garages and storage areas.
  2. Start with large, high-value items like furniture, electronics, and appliances.
  3. Flag expensive or sentimental items so they are clearly identified.
  4. Add boxed items under the correct room as packing happens.
  5. Label every box with the room name and box number to match your list.
  6. Before pack-out, confirm all rooms and high-value items are captured.

Tip: Keep labels short and consistent so movers can match boxes quickly.
